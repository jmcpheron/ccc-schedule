How to Use CCC Schedule
Search and Filters
  • Use the search box to find courses by keyword, course number, or title
  • Select specific terms, colleges, or subjects from the dropdowns
  • Filter by instruction mode, units, days, and time
  • Check "Open Sections Only" to see only available sections
View Modes
  • Card View: Shows detailed course information in cards
  • Table View: Compact view for browsing many courses
Section Information

Click on any section to view detailed information including:

  • Meeting times and locations
  • Instructor information
  • Enrollment status
  • Textbook requirements
